HOW TO APPLY

Open a new savings account online, anytime, anywhere at your convenience. Simply use your SingPass to login and apply.

Receive a confirmation email for your approved account application. Follow the instructions to download the SM App in Apple Store or Google Play to create and activate your e-Wallet.

Your SM Prepaid MasterCard will be sent to your registered mailing address within 14 working days. Log in to your SM App, activate your card and start using!
